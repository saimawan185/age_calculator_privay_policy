Welcome to Age Calculator. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our services:

  • Locally stored data: Saved events (birthdays, anniversaries, reminders) are stored on your device using shared preferences and are accessible only by you.
  • Google Account information: If you choose to sign in with Google (a premium feature), we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google to identify your account.
  • Cloud-synced events: Premium subscribers who sign in with Google have their saved events stored securely in Firebase Firestore, linked to their Google account.
  • Purchase information: Subscription purchases are processed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not store your payment details.
  • Contact information: If you contact us via email or WhatsApp, we may receive your name, email, and message content, used solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Store and sync your saved events across devices (premium subscribers).
  • Verify your subscription status and unlock premium features.
  • Send local notifications for birthdays, anniversaries, and reminders.
  • Respond to support requests and feedback.
  • Improve the app and user experience.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Premium Subscriptions

Age Calculator offers optional premium subscriptions that unlock additional features:

Premium Features
  • Remove all advertisements from the app
  • Sign in with Google for cloud backup
  • Sync saved events across multiple devices via Firebase
  • Offline access to your synced events

Subscriptions are available on a monthly ($0.99/month) or yearly ($9.99/year) basis. Payments are processed securely by Google Play. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Google Play account settings.

Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat, a third-party service that helps us verify active subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data to provide this service. See their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

4. Google Sign-In & Cloud Sync

Google Sign-In is an optional feature available exclusively to premium subscribers. When you sign in with Google:

  • Your Google account name, email address, and profile photo are used to identify your account within the app.
  • Your saved events are stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Your data is protected by Firebase security rules that ensure only you can read or write your own events.
  • You can sign out at any time, which stops cloud sync. Your locally stored events remain on your device.
  • You can request deletion of your cloud data at any time through the Account settings in the app.

We use Firebase (by Google) for authentication and cloud storage. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

5. Advertising

The free version of Age Calculator displays advertisements served by Google AdMob and may include mediation through Unity Ads. These services may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Premium subscribers have all advertisements removed.

For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, visit policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play: For app distribution and subscription billing.
  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and cloud data storage.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and verification.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (free users only).
  • Unity Ads: For ad mediation (free users only).
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Cloud data is stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules ensuring only authenticated users can access their own data.
  • All communication with Firebase uses HTTPS encryption.
  • We do not store your Google credentials — authentication is handled entirely by Google.
  • Local data is stored in Android's shared preferences, protected by the operating system's app sandbox.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Local data: Can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
  • Cloud data: Signed-in premium users can delete all cloud-synced events and their account data through Account Settings → Delete Account in the app. This permanently removes all your data from Firebase Firestore.
  • Google Sign-In access: Can be revoked at any time from your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you sign in with Google or purchase a subscription, you additionally consent to the collection and processing of data as described in Sections 3 and 4.
